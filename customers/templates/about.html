About Cakes by Brenda

Who We Are

At Cakes by Brenda, we take pride in creating exceptional cakes and pastries that make every celebration unforgettable. Established in Naivasha, Kenya, our mission is to bake with love and deliver joy to our customers through quality and creativity.

From birthdays to weddings, we specialize in crafting custom cakes that meet your unique needs. With a dedicated team of bakers and decorators, we guarantee fresh, flavorful, and beautifully designed cakes every time.

Our Mission

To spread happiness and sweetness in every bite by creating cakes that bring people together for memorable moments.

Our Vision

To be the leading bakery in Kenya, recognized for our innovative designs, quality products, and exceptional service.

Our Values

We value creativity, quality, customer satisfaction, and community engagement in all that we do.
